수동태 시제별 예문

1. 단순 현재

Form: is/are + past participle

Active Voice Passive Voice 한국어 번역
I clean the room every day. The room is cleaned every day. 방은 매일 청소돼요.
Students read books in the library. Books are read by students in the library. 학생들은 도서관에서 책을 읽어요.
Many people drink coffee in the morning. Coffee is drunk by many people in the morning. 많은 사람들이 아침에 커피를 마셔요.
I wash the car every weekend. The car is washed every weekend. 차는 매주 주말에 세차돼요.

2. 단순 과거

Form: was/were + past participle

Active Voice Passive Voice 한국어 번역
I cleaned the room yesterday. The room was cleaned yesterday. 방은 어제 청소됐어요.
The kids did the homework last night. The homework was done by the kids last night. 아이들이 어젯밤 숙제를 했어요.
My mom baked the cake. The cake was baked by my mom. 엄마가 케이크를 구웠어요.
We watched the movie last weekend. The movie was watched by us last weekend. 우리는 지난 주말에 영화를 봤어요.

3. 단순 미래

Form: will be + past participle

Active Voice Passive Voice 한국어 번역
I will clean the room tomorrow. The room will be cleaned tomorrow. 방은 내일 청소될 거예요.
The team will finish the project. The project will be finished by the team. 팀이 프로젝트를 끝낼 거예요.
I will wash the dishes after dinner. The dishes will be washed after dinner. 저녁 식사 후에 설거지가 될 거예요.
I will send the email later. The email will be sent by me later. 내가 나중에 이메일을 보낼 거예요.

4. 현재 진행

Form: is/are being + past participle

Active Voice Passive Voice 한국어 번역
I am cleaning the room right now. The room is being cleaned right now. 방은 지금 청소되고 있어요.
They are painting the house this week. The house is being painted this week. 집은 이번 주에 페인트칠되고 있어요.
My dad is cooking the food. The food is being cooked by my dad. 아빠가 지금 음식을 요리하고 있어요.
The team is writing the report. The report is being written by the team. 팀이 지금 보고서를 쓰고 있어요.

5. 과거 진행

Form: was/were being + past participle

Active Voice Passive Voice 한국어 번역
I was cleaning the room when you arrived. The room was being cleaned when I arrived. 내가 도착했을 때 방이 청소되고 있었어요.
They were repairing the car all day. The car was being repaired all day. 차는 하루 종일 수리되고 있었어요.
The choir was singing the song then. The song was being sung by the choir then. 그때 합창단이 노래를 부르고 있었어요.
I was washing the dishes at that time. The dishes were being washed at that time. 그때 설거지가 되고 있었어요.

6. 현재완료

Form: have/has been + past participle

Active Voice Passive Voice 한국어 번역
I have just cleaned the room. The room has just been cleaned. 방은 방금 청소됐어요.
Many people have read the book. The book has been read by many people. 그 책은 많은 사람들이 읽었어요.
I have mopped the floor this morning. The floor has been mopped this morning. 바닥은 오늘 아침에 닦였어요.
My friend has sent the letter. The letter has been sent by my friend. 친구가 편지를 보냈어요.

7. 과거완료

Form: had been + past participle

Active Voice Passive Voice 한국어 번역
I had cleaned the room before the party. The room had been cleaned before the party. 파티 전에 방이 청소돼 있었어요.
The kids had finished the homework. The homework had been finished by the kids. 아이들이 숙제를 끝내 놓았었어요.
We had eaten the cake before you arrived. The cake had been eaten before we arrived. 우리가 도착하기 전에 케이크가 먹혀 있었어요.
They had fixed the car before the trip. The car had been fixed before the trip. 여행 전에 차가 고쳐져 있었어요.

8. 미래완료

Form: will have been + past participle

Active Voice Passive Voice 한국어 번역
I will have cleaned the room by tomorrow. The room will have been cleaned by tomorrow. 내일까지 방이 청소돼 있을 거예요.
The team will have finished the project by next week. The project will have been finished by next week. 다음 주까지 팀이 프로젝트를 끝내 놓을 거예요.
I will have washed the dishes by tonight. The dishes will have been washed by tonight. 오늘 밤까지 설거지가 돼 있을 거예요.
My friend will have sent the email by noon. The email will have been sent by noon. 정오까지 친구가 이메일을 보내 놓을 거예요.
